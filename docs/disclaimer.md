## Summary

HookCheck is a **decision-support tool**, not a guarantee of safety.

- A "Likely Safe" result does not mean content is definitively safe.
- A "Likely Phishing" result does not mean content is definitively malicious.
- Newly registered phishing domains may not yet appear in VirusTotal.
- AI analysis can be fooled by adversarial messages.
- The tool does not browse URLs or render web pages.
- No liability is accepted for harm arising from acting on or ignoring analysis results.
- Use of this tool is at your own risk.
